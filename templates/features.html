What Makes This Different

01

AI

AI-Based Food Recognition

Advanced MobileNetV2 deep learning model trained on 7 food categories. Upload any food image and get instant recognition with confidence scores.

02

QT

Quantity-Based Nutrition Analysis

Switch between Per Serving and Custom grams. The dashboard updates calories, protein, fat, carbs, sugar, and sodium in real time using the selected quantity.

03

HW

ICMR/WHO Health Classification

Rule-based Healthy/Moderate/Unhealthy verdict from protein, fat, carbs, sugar, and sodium. Includes per-nutrient reasoning table and dairy-specific exceptions.

04

RG

Smart Recommendations

Recommendations are generated from nutrients marked Moderate or Unhealthy, giving targeted suggestions instead of generic tips.

05

S0

Formula-Based Health Score (0-100)

Explainable score using macro calorie percentages, sugar and sodium penalties, and carb balance. Shows component breakdown and applies dairy penalty exceptions where relevant.

06

DI

Daily Intake Tracker + Visual Charts

WHO 2000 kcal-based daily intake progress bars for all nutrients with exceed-limit alerts, plus quantity-aware interactive nutrition chart visualization.
